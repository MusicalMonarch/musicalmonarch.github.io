iDec Weather

  • Website Name: iDec Weather
  • Site Purpose: iDec's (pronounced I-Deck, based on the acronym for "I Don't Even Care") purpose is to provide an entertaining but reliable source for information about the weather. With a sarcastic take on weather-related subjects, visitors can approach the subject of weather in a light-hearted manner. We also aim to help visitors feel more prepared to take on any weather, saying, "I don't even care."
  • Domain Name: idecweather.com
