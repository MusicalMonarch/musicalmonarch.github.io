Target Audience

The general target audience is full of people looking to prepare themselves for the next week, next day, or even the next hour. We aim to serve those that want a simple telling of the weather patterns and what it will mean for their plans. Our target audience ranges from teenagers to middle-aged adults. Our target audience contains people that want a quick and general view of weather-related information.

Persona One

  • Fictional Name: Melanie Juarez
  • Major Responsibilities: College senior, intern at Google
  • Demographics: 22 years old, single, first generation college student, three younger siblings, major in marketing
  • Tasks and Environment: She's goal-oriented and ambitious in her field. She works and studies hard everyday, but she spends a lot of time on transportation due to the locations of her school and her work and her home.
  • Devices: Phone, laptop, personal computer

Persona Two

  • Fictional Name: Shelby Brown
  • Major Responsibilities: Stay-at-home Mother, Relief Society First Counselor in her ward
  • Demographics: 40 years old, married, mother of five children, has a Bachelors in Psychology
  • Tasks and Environment: She stays at home most of the time to take care of the house and her children. She helps them with homework and gets them ready each day for school. She also has meetings with her Relief Society members.
  • Devices: Phone, laptop

Scenarios

  • Melanie has to check the weather for the week so that she can plan her route of transportation to each location needs to go to. "Can this website tell me the weather for the week?"
  • Shelby wants to see if it'll rain this week so she can prepare her kids with umbrellas. "Will it rain this week?"
  • Shelby's preparing a birthday party for one of her children. She wants to see if it will be too hot on his birthday. "What will the temperature be on this day?"
  • Shelby is planning a vacation with her family to a specific location. She knows it will be cold in this location, so she wants to be prepared for it. "How do I prepare for cold weather?"
  • Melanie has a class project, and she needs to record the history of the weather in the past weeks and the predicted weather for the next week. "What is the history of the weather in this location?"
