Color Scheme

  • Primary: #E0EFDE
  • Secondary: #E08D79
  • Accent: #B3F2DD
  • Tertiary: #49416D

The primary color will go to the entire body of the webpage. The secondary color will go to the background of headers and footers. The accent will be the color for headings and other important elements like links. The tertiary will be an optional color for hovered elements and other unimportant elements.
